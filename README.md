# 🚘 BMW Executive Summary Dashboard

A **premium multi-page business intelligence dashboard** built using **Python, Tkinter, Pandas, and Matplotlib** to analyze BMW global sales performance.

This project transforms raw CSV sales data into an **interactive executive analytics suite** with KPI cards, revenue trends, regional intelligence, model insights, segment performance, and engine trend analysis.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This dashboard was designed to help leadership and business teams analyze:

* 📈 **Revenue trends (2019–2024)**
* 🚗 **2024 sales performance**
* 🌍 **Top-performing countries**
* 🏆 **Top revenue-generating BMW models**
* ⚡ **Segment-wise intelligence (Electric, SUV, Sedan)**
* 🔋 **Engine trend evolution**
* 📊 **Regional revenue performance**
* 🥧 **Model revenue contribution mix**

The interface follows a **BMW-inspired dark premium theme** with blue accent highlights.

---

## ✨ Features

### 📊 Page 1 — Executive Dashboard

* KPI cards for 2024 revenue, units sold, top country, and top model
* Revenue trend line chart (2019–2024)
* Monthly sales trend chart
* Top 5 countries revenue chart
* Top 6 models by units
* Revenue by segment donut chart
* Executive summary insights

### 🌍 Page 2 — Regional Performance

* Country-wise revenue table (2020–2024)
* YoY growth and business status
* Trend line chart for top 5 countries

### 🚘 Page 3 — Model Performance Intelligence

* Top 10 models ranked by 2024 revenue
* Revenue share %, YoY growth, and performance tiers
* Donut chart for model revenue contribution

### ⚡ Page 4 — Segment Intelligence

* Separate tables for:

  * Electric
  * SUV
  * Sedan
* Model-wise sales and revenue

### 🔋 Page 5 — Engine Trends

* Multi-line chart showing engine popularity by units sold
* Tracks trend changes from 2019–2024
